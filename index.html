The Challenge Facing American Agriculture

Industrial food systems have disconnected us from the source of our nutrition, creating barriers between farming families and the communities they serve.

01

Lost Connections

Families have no relationship with the farmers who produce their food, losing the trust and transparency that comes from knowing your source.

02

Diminished Quality

Mass distribution systems prioritize shelf life over freshness, resulting in eggs that have lost their nutritional peak by the time they reach your table.

03

Farmer Struggles

Small family farms receive minimal compensation from industrial buyers, making it difficult to maintain sustainable, ethical farming practices.

04

Limited Variety

Corporate agriculture focuses on standardization, eliminating access to heritage breeds and specialty varieties that offer unique flavors and nutrition.
