Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the eggs are fresh?

All eggs on our marketplace come directly from local farmers who prioritize freshness and quality. Each farmer provides detailed information about their practices, and eggs are typically shipped within 1-2 days of being laid.

What makes Harvest Eggs different?

We cut out the middleman, connecting you directly with farmers. This means fresher eggs for you and fair compensation for farmers - they get more than 90% of the cost listed right into their pockets.

How does shipping work?

Shipping costs vary based on location and order size. We work with reliable shipping partners to ensure your eggs arrive safely and quickly. Farmers handle packaging with care to maintain freshness during transit.

Can I set up recurring orders?

Yes! Our subscription service allows you to set up weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly deliveries of your favorite eggs. You can modify or cancel your subscription at any time.

What if I have an issue with my order?

Contact us immediately at admin@harvesteggs.com. While we act as a connector between buyers and farmers, we're committed to helping resolve any issues and ensuring you have a positive experience.
